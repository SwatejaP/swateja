Smart Indoor Parking System

FUNCTIONALITY:

The module discusses the priority to find and provide the nearest parking lot to the entrance for the user. This ensures that the driver doesnâ€™t waste their time searching the vacant parking and he also can obtain the nearest parking to the entrance with the help of the system.

ALGORITHM USED:

Dijkstra Algorithm

The procedure

  • As the car enters the parking area a unique number is assigned to the car.
  • An array named parkingLots stores the parking slot numbers which are not yet occupied.
  • Using Dijkstra Algorithm we find the nearest parking slot available to that particular car.
  • The car is assigned with a parking slot such that it is near to parking enterance as well inorder to avoid the conjestion.
  • Once a parking slot is allocated to a car it is marked as seen/occupied until the car in that slot exists the parking area.
  • design principles applicable : Edge Relaxation.
  • design technique: greedy algorithm.
  • Time complexity: Since priority queue is used,the time complexity is O(V+E)logV)
  • data structure used: Priority Queue.
